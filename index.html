📝 Tips & Notes

  • Housing app deadline: Submit by May 1, 2026 for guaranteed 4-year housing.
  • Wear comfortable shoes — campus is hilly and you'll walk a lot.
  • Bring a water bottle — free refill stations across campus.
  • Parking: Structures 4 & 7 (Sunset Blvd exit) for north campus; Structures 2 & 8 (Wilshire exit) for south. Shuttle runs 8 AM–4:30 PM.
  • Look for yellow-shirt volunteers with "Ask Me" signs if you get lost.
  • No official check-in — the Admission Tent is your starting point but it's casual.
  • Most 30-min presentations repeat 2–3× during the day, so if you miss one, check for a later slot.
  • The Information Fair closes at 3 PM — don't leave it for last.
